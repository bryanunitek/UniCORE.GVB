## Scope

This statement defines how the **UniCORE.GVB** name and the **`UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>`** classified names may and may not be used in public, and sets binding rules for the UniCORE.GVB GitHub Discussions space.

The canonical programme-wide statement is published in the UniVERSE repository at [`STATEMENT-ON-CLAIMS.md`](https://github.com/bryanunitek/UniVERSE/blob/main/STATEMENT-ON-CLAIMS.md). The UniCORE-name statement is published at [`UniCORE/STATEMENT-ON-CLAIMS.md`](https://github.com/bryanunitek/UniCORE/blob/main/STATEMENT-ON-CLAIMS.md). This file extends the same rules to the **UniCORE.GVB** name and the open list of industry-classified names.

---

## What UniCORE.GVB is

UniCORE.GVB is the **substrate-services layer** of the programme — the Global Virtual Bridge. It provides the production-grade infrastructure (mail, file transfer, DNS, federation, tenancy, topology, FTP, Outlook integration, web disk, calendar/contacts, server profiles, node operations, bandwidth and storage reporting, jurisdictional posture, inter-node bridging) that any Vertical Solution may run on top of.

It is **one codebase** that is **classified for specific industries**, parameterised by the [UniCORE AI 12-Level Governance Model](https://github.com/bryanunitek/UniCORE-AI) for the industry it serves.

UniCORE.GVB is a governance work, not a product. It is not a platform. It is not a SaaS offering. It is not a tool category. It is not a brand for sale.

It is given, not sold. It is irrevocable.

This statement governs the use of the name **UniCORE.GVB** and the classified names **`UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>`** as they are used by **Unitek Systems Limited** from their first use in commerce in May 2026 and thereafter. It is a forward-looking statement.

Where this statement refers to **UniCORE.GVB**, it means the substrate-services layer as published by Unitek Systems Limited from May 2026.

The list is **open** and is defined as the programme expands. New classifications are added under the rule recorded in [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md). Each classification name is in scope of this statement when added.

### Military is intentionally absent

**Military is not in the list and will not be added.** The UniCORE Positioning Principle is **Harmony, Peace, Space Exploration, for Humanity**. Industry classifications must align with this positioning; Military uses do not, by structural choice. This is not a marketing posture — it is a programme-level commitment recorded across the canonical material.

Any attempt to publish, market, or otherwise circulate a `UniCORE.GVB.Military`, `UniCORE.GVB.Defence`, `UniCORE.GVB.Defense`, `UniCORE.GVB.Weapons`, or any similarly framed classification — by anyone, including Unitek Systems Limited and its group subsidiaries — is **outside the scope of this statement** and does not carry programme authorisation. Such names cannot be presented as official UniCORE.GVB classifications.

## Claims that are not permitted

No person, company, consortium, standards body, regulator, or research group may:

- Represent UniCORE.GVB, or any `UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>` classification, as their own framework, product, platform, or service.
- Sell, licence, or sublicense the UniCORE.GVB name or any `UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>` name. These names are protected by trademark and are not available for resale or sublicensing.
- Claim proprietary ownership of the substrate-services layer or the industry-classification pattern. The substrate is released under CC BY 4.0 once certification is recorded; the classification pattern is part of the canonical programme material.
- Offer "UniCORE.GVB" or "UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>" certification, compliance, accreditation, partnership, or endorsement services without written authority from Unitek Systems Limited.
- Market a product, service, or agent as "UniCORE.GVB-aligned", "UniCORE.GVB-compliant", "UniCORE.GVB-powered", "UniCORE.GVB-certified", or any variation implying official status — without written authority from Unitek Systems Limited.
- Use the UniCORE.GVB name, or any classification name, in a way that suggests the author or Unitek Systems Limited endorses the user, the user's product, or the user's claims.
- Publish a `UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>` classification not on the official open list, under a name that suggests official programme status, without written authority from Unitek Systems Limited.
- Publish a `UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>` classification for any industry that does not align with the UniCORE Positioning Principle (Harmony / Peace / Space Exploration / for Humanity) — including but not limited to Military, Defence, Weapons, or any classification whose primary use case is the production of harm.

These restrictions do not prevent:

- **Implementation** of substrate-services that satisfy the TrueAI Foundation invariants, the UniCORE AI architecture, and the published UniCORE.GVB substrate (once published). Implementation is encouraged.
- **Commercial work** performed around such an implementation — integration, training, support, extensions outside the core. This is permitted, subject to the gift principle and the monopoly clause in [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md).
- **Hosted-service operation** by anyone — including Unitek Systems USA Inc — under the same CC BY 4.0 licence available to everyone. There is no privileged operator tier.
- **Academic, policy, and public-interest citation** of UniCORE.GVB. Welcomed, subject to CC BY 4.0 attribution.
- **Public challenge, correction, or stress-testing** of UniCORE.GVB on the Discussions tab.
- **Independent producer work** on substrate-services for industries not yet on the official classification list, under CC BY 4.0, provided the producer does not present their work as the official UniCORE.GVB classification for that industry without written authority.

## Application to GitHub Discussions

This statement is a binding rule of the UniCORE.GVB Discussions space.

In Discussions on this repository, participants may not:

- Post threads or replies that present UniCORE.GVB as a product, platform, SaaS offering, agent, or tool category.
- Post threads or replies that advertise, market, or solicit business for "UniCORE.GVB" services, certification, partnership, or compliance.
- Post threads or replies that use the UniCORE.GVB name to suggest endorsement of any external product, service, or organisation.
- Post threads or replies that propose Military, Defence, Weapons, or similarly framed classifications as if they were in scope of the programme.
- Post threads or replies that misclassify UniCORE.GVB in a way that invites confusion with a commercial offering.

Threads and replies of this kind will be removed without notice. Repeat behaviour will lead to participation being withdrawn.

This is not a restriction on debate. Challenge, architectural critique, edge-case analysis, translation, correction, classification proposals (within the Positioning Principle), and adaptation under CC BY 4.0 remain welcome and are the primary purpose of the Discussions space.

## Why this matters

UniCORE.GVB is the substrate-services layer of a governance foundation for the critical path of consequential decisions made by or with AI systems. If its name becomes a marketing surface, the foundation under it stops being load-bearing. The Positioning Principle keeps the substrate from being deployed against the very humans the gift is for; turning it into a weapons-class delivery layer would invert the gift principle. The boundary is structural, not stylistic.
