The UniCORE.GVB Discussions space inherits the foundation rules and adds the natural scope of the substrate-services layer:

- **Substrate-services questions** about mail, file transfer, DNS, federation, tenancy, topology, FTP, Outlook integration, web disk, calendar/contacts, server profiles, node operations, bandwidth and storage reporting, jurisdictional posture, and inter-node bridging — the documented scope of UniCORE.GVB.
