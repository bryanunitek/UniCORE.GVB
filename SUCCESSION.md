## Scope extension to UniCORE.GVB

The UniCORE.GVB name surface and the open list of `UniCORE.GVB.<Industry>` substrate-services-layer classifications inherit the succession arrangement set out in the canonical document.

Two structural points carry through to this repository:

1. **The gift principle is not subject to succession.** The CC BY 4.0 licence has already been given for the documentation surface published here. Succession concerns the stewardship of the name, the structural rules, and the published documentation, not the gift surface itself.
2. **The substrate-services layer is part of the gift triad's natural extension.** The same succession arrangement that protects UniVERSE, TrueAI, and UniCORE-AI extends here.
